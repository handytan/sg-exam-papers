Question 1

2 marks

What is the common characteristic of all birds?

  • They can fly.
  • They have three body parts.
  • They reproduce by laying eggs.
  • Their young do not look like the adults.

Answer

Option 3. All birds reproduce by laying eggs — this is the one characteristic shared by all birds. Not all birds can fly (e.g. penguins, ostriches). Birds are insects that have three body parts (head, thorax, abdomen) — birds have two main body parts. Some birds have young that look similar to adults (precocial birds). Only egg-laying is universal to all birds.

(c)

What would happen to the volume of water in the graph from D to E? Give a reason. [1]

Answer

AB, BC, and CD show heat gain by the content in the pan. During AB, the ice gains heat and its temperature rises.
