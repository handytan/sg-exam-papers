Question 1

2 marks

Which of the following statements about how living things obtain energy are correct?

A   Fungi can make their own food to obtain energy.
B   Plant-eaters obtain energy by eating other plants.
C   Plants make use of the food that they made for energy.
D   Plant-and-animal eaters obtain their energy only directly from plants.

  • A and C only
  • B and C only
  • B and D only
  • A, C and D only

Answer

Option 2 (B and C only). B is correct — plant-eaters (herbivores) obtain energy by eating plants. C is correct — plants make their own food by photosynthesis and use that food for energy. A is wrong — fungi cannot make their own food; they are non-green and obtain food from dead/living matter. D is wrong — plant-and-animal eaters (omnivores) get energy from both plants and animals, not only directly from plants.

Question 8

Based on the food web above, which organisms are both predators as well as prey?

  • frog only
  • frog and snake only
  • hawk and snake only
  • grasshopper, mouse and rabbit only

Answer

Option 2 (frog and snake only). An organism that is both predator and prey must eat another animal (predator) and be eaten by another animal (prey). The frog eats the grasshopper (predator) and is eaten by the snake (prey) ✔. The snake eats the frog and mouse (predator) and is eaten by the hawk (prey) ✔. The hawk is a top predator — it eats but is not eaten, so it is not prey. The grasshopper, mouse and rabbit eat only the plant (they are not predators of animals), so although they are prey they are not predators.

Answer
