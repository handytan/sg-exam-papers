Question 1

2 marks

The diagrams show two animals.

How are the animals similar?

  1. lay eggs
  2. have scales
  3. have moist skin
  4. have a four-stage life cycle

Answer

Option 1. Both a frog (an amphibian) and a grasshopper (an insect) reproduce by laying eggs, so that is how they are similar. Neither animal has scales, so option 2 is wrong. Only the frog has moist skin (the grasshopper does not), so option 3 is not a shared trait. Neither has a four-stage life cycle – the frog develops egg → tadpole → adult and the grasshopper egg → nymph → adult – so option 4 is wrong.

Question 20

When water changes from gas to liquid at 100 °C, which of the following is correct?

  2. The water gains heat from the surroundings.
  3. The water loses heat to the surroundings.

Answer

(b) The white solid cannot be seen after a while. Explain why. [1]
