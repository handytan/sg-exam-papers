Question 1

2 marks

Which of the following organisms can make its own food?

  • fern
  • yeast
  • mould
  • mushroom

Answer

Option 1. Ferns are plants that can carry out photosynthesis to make their own food using sunlight, water and carbon dioxide. Yeast, mould and mushrooms are fungi — they cannot make their own food and must absorb nutrients from other sources.
