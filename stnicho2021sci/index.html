Question 1

Benny found the pupa of organism Z on a tree branch as shown below.

Which of the following could organism Z be?

  • butterfly
  • mosquito
  • cockroach
  • grasshopper

Answer

Option 1. A butterfly undergoes complete metamorphosis with a pupa (chrysalis) stage. Mosquitoes have a pupa stage too, but mosquito pupae are aquatic (found in water), not on tree branches. Cockroaches and grasshoppers undergo incomplete metamorphosis and do not have a pupa stage at all.

Which of the following processes is the bird helping out with?

  • fertilisation
  • germination
  • pollination
  • seed dispersal

Answer

Option 3. When a bird flies from flower to flower, it carries pollen grains on its body and transfers them from the anther of one flower to the stigma of another flower. Fertilisation happens inside the flower after pollination; germination is the sprouting of seeds; seed dispersal involves seeds being carried away from the parent plant.

  • shadow
  • A only
  • A and C only
  • B and D only
  • B, C and D only

Answer

Option 3. Matter is anything that has mass and occupies space. Light is electromagnetic radiation and has no mass — it is not matter. A shadow is simply the absence of light where an opaque object blocks it — it has no mass and takes up no space, so it is not matter. Air is a gas made of molecules (has mass and occupies space) — it IS matter. Clouds are made of tiny water droplets or ice crystals (have mass and occupy space) — they ARE matter.
