D   Both have the same type of body covering.

  • B only
  • A and D only
  • B and C only

Answer

Option 4. A is incorrect: birds have two body parts (head and body/trunk) while insects have three body parts (head, thorax, abdomen). The warm moist air from above the ground enters the underground pipe. Underground, the temperature is much cooler (7°C–16°C).
